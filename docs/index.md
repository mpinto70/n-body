# Multiparticle gravity system

This system was inspired by [these articles](https://wordpress.com/read/blogs/158040751/posts/26)


Equations of motion:

$$
s = s_0 + v \cdot t + a \frac{t^2}{2} \\
v = v_0 + a \cdot t \\
F = m \cdot a \\
F = G \frac {m_1 \cdot m_2}{r^2}
$$
